## 1. LETTUCE - BOSTON (Butterhead)

### Basic Information
- **Scientific Name:** Lactuca sativa L. var. capitata (Butterhead type)
- **Common Names:** Boston Lettuce, Butterhead Lettuce, Butter Lettuce
- **Family:** Asteraceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic

### Growth Cycle
- **Germination:** 7-10 days (65-75°F)
- **Transplant Age:** 3-4 weeks
- **Days to Harvest:** 55-75 days from seed
- **Total Cycle:** 60-80 days
- **Notes:** Takes longer than leaf lettuce, shorter than Iceberg

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.25 kg (per head)
- **Expected Waste:** 15%

### Fertilizer Schedule
**Pre-plant:**
- Balanced NPK (10-10-10)

**Hydroponic:**
- N: 150 ppm, P: 50 ppm, K: 200 ppm
- Ca: 175 ppm, Mg: 45 ppm, S: 45 ppm
- Micronutrients: Fe 3 ppm, Mn 0.5 ppm, B 0.5 ppm, Zn 0.3 ppm, Cu 0.1 ppm, Mo 0.1 ppm

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 5.8-6.0 (optimal 5.8)
- **EC:** 2.0 mS/cm (1.8-2.2 range acceptable)
- **Temperature:** 60-70°F (15-21°C)
- **Humidity:** 50-70%
- **Light:** 14-16 hours, DLI 12-17 mol/m²/day
- **Spacing:** 8-10 inches (20-25 cm)

### Common Pests & Diseases
**Same as other lettuces:**
1. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity) - Virus vectors
2. **Downy Mildew** (HIGH severity) - Bremia lactucae
3. **Lettuce Drop** (HIGH severity) - Sclerotinia
4. **Tipburn** (MEDIUM severity) - Calcium deficiency
5. **Gray Mold/Botrytis** (MEDIUM severity)

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Carrot, Radish, Strawberry, Cucumber, Onion
- **Incompatible:** Celery, Cabbage, Parsley

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** Cool season - November to March
- **Planting Months:** November, December, January, February
- **Harvest Months:** January, February, March, April
- **Heat Tolerance:** VERY LOW (bolts quickly in heat)
- **Water Requirement:** HIGH
- **Greenhouse:** REQUIRED for UAE
- **Notes:** "Butterhead type with tender, sweet leaves. More delicate than crisphead. Excellent for hydroponics. Climate-controlled greenhouse essential. Premium product for salad mixes. Quick-growing for fresh market."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 15-20/kg
- **Labor Hours:** 0.3 hours/plant

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Loose but formed head, tender leaves, no tipburn, 200-300g
- **Grade B:** Slightly open head, minor tipburn OK, 150-200g

## 2. LETTUCE - FRISEE (Curly Endive)

### Basic Information
- **Scientific Name:** Cichorium endivia var. crispum (NOT Lactuca sativa)
- **Common Names:** Frisée, Curly Endive, Chicorée Frisée
- **Family:** Asteraceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic
- **Note:** Technically endive (Cichorium), not lettuce (Lactuca), but grouped with lettuce commercially

### Growth Cycle
- **Germination:** 7-14 days
- **Days to Harvest:** 45-100 days depending on variety
  - Fast varieties: 45-60 days
  - Standard varieties: 80-90 days
- **Total Cycle:** 50-100 days

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.3 kg
- **Expected Waste:** 20% (outer leaves removed)

### Fertilizer Schedule
**Similar to lettuce:**
- Pre-plant: Balanced 10-10-10
- Growing: Higher nitrogen for leaf production
- Endive requires fertile soil

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 5.8-6.2
- **EC:** 2.0-2.5 mS/cm
- **Temperature:** 60-65°F (15-18°C) optimal
- **Humidity:** 50-70%
- **Light:** 12-14 hours
- **Spacing:** 10-12 inches

### Common Pests & Diseases
**Similar to lettuce:**
1. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity)
2. **Downy Mildew** (MEDIUM-HIGH severity)
3. **Bottom Rot** (MEDIUM severity)
4. **Slugs and Snails** (MEDIUM severity - outdoor)

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Similar to lettuce - carrots, radish, beets
- **Incompatible:** Similar to lettuce

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** Cool season - November to March
- **Planting Months:** November, December, January, February
- **Harvest Months:** January, February, March, April
- **Heat Tolerance:** LOW (slightly more heat-tolerant than lettuce, but still cool-season)
- **Water Requirement:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Greenhouse:** REQUIRED for UAE
- **Notes:** "Specialty salad green with slightly bitter taste. More heat-tolerant than regular lettuce. Used in gourmet salads. Hearts can be blanched for milder flavor. Growing interest in UAE fine dining. Climate control essential."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 25-35/kg (premium specialty green)
- **Labor Hours:** 0.3 hours/plant

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Bright green-yellow curled leaves, crisp, no browning, 250-350g
- **Grade B:** Minor browning acceptable, 200-250g

## 3. LETTUCE - LOLLO (Lollo Bionda & Lollo Rosso)

### Basic Information
- **Scientific Name:** Lactuca sativa var. crispa
- **Common Names:** Lollo Bionda (green), Lollo Rosso (red), Coral Lettuce
- **Family:** Asteraceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic
- **Varieties:** Lollo Bionda (bright green frilly), Lollo Rosso (red/copper frilly)

### Growth Cycle
- **Germination:** 7-10 days
- **Days to Harvest:** 45-55 days from seed
- **Total Cycle:** 50-60 days
- **Notes:** Fast-growing loose-leaf type

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.2 kg (loose-leaf harvest)
- **Expected Waste:** 10%

### Fertilizer Schedule
**Hydroponic:**
- Resh solution: 5 mM KNO3, 5 mM Ca(NO3)2, 2 mM MgSO4, 1 mM KH2PO4
- Micronutrients: 7 μM MnCl2, 0.7 μM ZnSO4, 0.8 μM CuSO4
- Main requirement: Nitrogen, with moderate K and P

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 5.8-6.2
- **EC:** 1.8-2.2 mS/cm
- **Temperature:** 60-70°F (15-21°C)
- **Humidity:** 50-70%
- **Light:** Red/blue LED combination increases yield in NFT systems
- **DLI:** 12-17 mol/m²/day
- **Spacing:** 8-10 inches, 16-20 plants/m²

### Common Pests & Diseases
**Same as other leaf lettuces:**
1. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity)
2. **Downy Mildew** (MEDIUM-HIGH severity)
3. **Gray Mold** (MEDIUM severity)
4. **Tipburn** (LOW-MEDIUM severity - less prone than heading types)

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Carrot, Radish, Strawberry, Beets
- **Incompatible:** Celery, Parsley

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** Cool season - November to March
- **Planting Months:** November, December, January, February, March
- **Harvest Months:** December, January, February, March, April
- **Heat Tolerance:** LOW (similar to other lettuces)
- **Water Requirement:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Greenhouse:** REQUIRED for UAE
- **Notes:** "Beautiful decorative lettuce - green (Lollo Bionda) or red (Lollo Rosso). Very popular in hydroponic production. Fast-growing (45-55 days). Can harvest as cut-and-come-again at 2-3 inches or whole head. Excellent for salad mixes and garnish. Premium appearance. Climate control essential for UAE."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 20-28/kg (premium for red variety)
- **Labor Hours:** 0.25 hours/plant

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Bright color (green or red), tightly curled frilly leaves, crisp, no damage, 180-250g
- **Grade B:** Minor color variation, slight browning acceptable, 150-180g

## 5. LETTUCE - RADICCHIO

- **Common Names:** Radicchio, Italian Chicory, Red Chicory
- **Family:** Asteraceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic
- **Note:** Technically chicory (Cichorium), not lettuce (Lactuca), but grouped with lettuce commercially

- **Days to Harvest:** 60-85 days

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.3 kg (compact head)
- **Expected Waste:** 20%

### Fertilizer Schedule
**Similar to endive:**
- Pre-plant: Balanced 10-10-10

- Less fertilizer needed than lettuce (avoid excess N which reduces color)

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 5.8-6.5
- **EC:** 2.0-2.5 mS/cm
- **Temperature:** 55-65°F (13-18°C) for best color development
- **Humidity:** 50-70%
- **Light:** 12-14 hours
- **Spacing:** 10-12 inches

### Common Pests & Diseases
**Similar to endive:**
1. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity)
2. **Downy Mildew** (MEDIUM severity)
3. **Bottom Rot** (MEDIUM severity)
4. **Tipburn** (LOW severity)

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Onions, Beans, Peas
- **Incompatible:** Similar to lettuce

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** Cool season - November to February (needs cold for best color)
- **Planting Months:** November, December, January
- **Harvest Months:** January, February, March
- **Heat Tolerance:** VERY LOW (needs cool temperatures for quality)
- **Water Requirement:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Greenhouse:** REQUIRED with climate control
- **Notes:** "Specialty Italian chicory with deep red/purple leaves and white ribs. Slightly bitter flavor popular in gourmet salads. Requires cool temperatures (55-65F) for best color development. Premium product for fine dining. Climate-controlled greenhouse essential. Challenging but high-value crop for UAE."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 30-45/kg (premium specialty)
- **Labor Hours:** 0.4 hours/plant

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Deep red-purple color, tight compact head, crisp white ribs, 250-350g
- **Grade B:** Lighter color acceptable, slightly loose head, 200-250g

## 6. MULUKHIYAH (Jute Mallow)

### Basic Information
- **Scientific Name:** Corchorus olitorius L.
- **Common Names:** Mulukhiyah, Molokhia, Jute Mallow, Jew's Mallow, Egyptian Spinach
- **Family:** Malvaceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic

### Growth Cycle
- **Germination:** 7-14 days (warm soil 70-85°F)
- **Days to Harvest:** 50-70 days for leaves (continuous harvest)
- **Total Cycle:** 60-80 days
- **Notes:** Cut-and-come-again harvesting extends production

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.8 kg over season (multiple cuttings)
- **Expected Waste:** 10%

### Fertilizer Schedule
**High nitrogen requirements:**
- Pre-plant: Compost or aged manure
- Growing: NPK 20-10-10 (higher nitrogen for leaf production)
- Frequency: Every 3-4 weeks during growth

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 6.0-6.8
- **EC:** 2.0-2.8 mS/cm
- **Temperature:** 70-90°F (21-32°C) - loves heat!
- **Humidity:** 50-70%
- **Light:** Full sun, 8-10 hours minimum
- **Spacing:** 6-8 inches (close spacing), 20-25 plants/m²

### Common Pests & Diseases
1. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Symptoms: Leaf curling, honeydew
   - Treatment: Insecticidal soap, neem oil

2. **Whiteflies** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Symptoms: Small white flies on underside of leaves
   - Treatment: Yellow sticky traps, insecticidal soap

3. **Root Rot** (MEDIUM severity - if overwatered)
   - Prevention: Well-drained soil, avoid overwatering
   - Treatment: Improve drainage

4. **Leaf Spot** (LOW-MEDIUM severity)
   - Symptoms: Brown spots on leaves
   - Prevention: Avoid overhead watering
   - Treatment: Remove infected leaves, fungicide if severe

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Corn, Beans, Okra (similar climate needs)
- **Incompatible:** Cool-season crops

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** EXCELLENT FOR UAE SUMMER! April to October
- **Planting Months:** April, May, June, July, August
- **Harvest Months:** May, June, July, August, September, October
- **Heat Tolerance:** VERY HIGH (loves heat 21-32°C, thrives in UAE summer!)
- **Water Requirement:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Greenhouse:** NOT needed - thrives outdoors in UAE heat
- **Notes:** "PERFECT FOR UAE SUMMER! Traditional Middle Eastern leafy green used in famous Egyptian dish. Heat-loving plant that thrives when most greens fail. Fast-growing (50-70 days). Cut-and-come-again harvest. Very popular in Arab cuisine. High local demand. Grows excellently in UAE summer heat. Can produce multiple cuttings. Essential traditional vegetable."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 15-25/kg (high demand in season)
- **Labor Hours:** 0.4 hours/plant

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Young tender leaves, bright green, 4-6 inches, no flowering
- **Grade B:** Slightly older leaves, minor discoloration OK

## 7. ZAATAR (Syrian Oregano)

### Basic Information
- **Scientific Name:** Origanum syriacum L.
- **Common Names:** Zaatar, Za'atar, Syrian Oregano, Bible Hyssop
- **Family:** Lamiaceae
- **Farm Type Compatibility:** open_field, greenhouse, hydroponic

### Growth Cycle
- **Germination:** 10-20 days
- **Establishment:** 90-120 days to mature plant
- **Days to First Harvest:** 90-120 days
- **Harvest Duration:** Perennial - continuous harvest year-round
- **Notes:** Perennial herb in warm climates

### Yield Information
- **Yield Per Plant:** 0.5 kg fresh leaves per year (multiple cuttings)
- **Expected Waste:** 15%

### Fertilizer Schedule
**Light feeder (typical for Mediterranean herbs):**
- Pre-plant: Light compost incorporation
- Growing: NPK 5-10-5 every 6-8 weeks
- Avoid excess nitrogen (reduces essential oil content and flavor)

### Hydroponic Parameters
- **pH:** 6.0-7.0 (prefers slightly alkaline)
- **EC:** 1.5-2.0 mS/cm (lower than most crops - light feeder)
- **Temperature:** 65-85°F (18-29°C)
- **Humidity:** 40-60% (prefers drier conditions)
- **Light:** Full sun, 6-8 hours minimum
- **Spacing:** 12-18 inches

### Common Pests & Diseases
**Generally pest-resistant (aromatic oils deter pests):**

1. **Aphids** (LOW severity - rare on aromatic herbs)
   - Treatment: Insecticidal soap if needed

2. **Spider Mites** (LOW-MEDIUM severity - in dry conditions)
   - Prevention: Occasional misting
   - Treatment: Neem oil

3. **Root Rot** (MEDIUM severity - if overwatered)
   - Prevention: Well-drained soil, avoid overwatering
   - Treatment: Improve drainage

4. **Powdery Mildew** (LOW severity - in humid conditions)
   - Prevention: Good air circulation, avoid overhead watering
   - Treatment: Sulfur fungicide

### Companion Planting
- **Beneficial:** Most vegetables (aromatic herbs deter pests), Tomatoes, Eggplant, Peppers
- **Incompatible:** None significant

### UAE Adaptations
- **Season:** Year-round in UAE! Best growth spring/fall
- **Planting Months:** March, April, May, September, October, November
- **Harvest Months:** Year-round (continuous)
- **Heat Tolerance:** HIGH (Mediterranean origin, adapted to hot/dry climates)
- **Water Requirement:** LOW-MEDIUM (drought-tolerant once established)
- **Greenhouse:** NOT needed - thrives outdoors
- **Notes:** "EXCELLENT FOR UAE! Traditional Middle Eastern herb essential in Arab cuisine. Heat and drought tolerant Mediterranean herb. Perennial in UAE climate - plant once, harvest for years. Used fresh or dried for famous zaatar spice blend. Very high demand in local markets. Low water needs. Aromatic oils deter pests. Can grow year-round with best growth spring/fall. Essential traditional herb."

### Economics
- **Market Price:** AED 40-60/kg fresh, AED 100-150/kg dried
- **Labor Hours:** 0.3 hours/plant per harvest cycle

### Quality Grading
- **Grade A:** Young tender leaves and flowering tops, strong aroma, no browning, fresh
- **Grade B:** Slightly older leaves, mild aroma acceptable
